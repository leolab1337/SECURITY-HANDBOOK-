Rights of Customers and Dr.Delivery



  1. Rights of Customers
      Customers have the right to:

    1. Requiring Dr.Delivery to provide content-based services at the scope of service and other agreed service delivery needs.

    2. Be assured of private information confidentiality and safety for information in the form of documents, packages, packages of goods.

    3. Customers have the right to complain about damaged, torn, damaged, lost items. The statute of limitations for complaints is specified as follows:

      1. 06 months from the end of the postal processing time, for complaints about the loss of postal items or delayed delivery of the items compared to the announced transit time; where Dr.Delivery does not disclose the transit time, this period shall be counted from the day after the date of acceptance of the item;

      2. 01 month from the date the postal item is delivered to the recipient, for complaints about the item being lost or damaged, about the freight rate and other matters directly related to the item.

    4. To be compensated for damage as agreed upon and in accordance with current postal laws.

    5. Ask Dr.Delivery for monthly billing statements and bills.

  2. Rights of Dr.Delivery
      Dr.De have the right to:

    1. R Request Customers to strictly comply with the requirements of the State and the guidance of the Ministry of Information and Communications on the above services.

    2. Dr.Delivery reserves the right to refuse delivery and any other service for items containing prohibited items or objects carrying state secrets for which Dr.Delivery is not authorized to receive or deliver. Dr.Delivery shall not be liable under any circumstances if Customer fails to notify Dr.Delivery for items containing state secrets and/or Customer has no authority or responsibility for such items.

    3. Dr.Delivery has the right to refuse or suspend to continue providing the service and the Customer is obliged to receive back the postal items that are documents and objects carrying state secrets that must be delivered through the service provider postal service designated by the state.

    4. Dr.Delivery has the right not to transport, deliver the item or have the right to inspect and handle the item or request the Customer to provide, supplement or correct information for the item in case the item lacks necessary information about postal items or postal items without records and full signatures of relevant parties as prescribed by law.

    5. Request the Customer to pack the goods (for fragile goods, frozen goods...) to ensure that the goods are safely preserved during transportation.

    6. Check the content of information in the form of documents (except in the case of letters), parcels, and packages of goods before accepting delivery (with the witness of the customer's sender).

    7. Have the right to refuse to perform the service for parcels containing goods on the list of items not accepted by Dr.Delivery, items with conditions but do not meet the conditions or in case the Customer does not provide sufficient documents and papers. prove that the goods requested by Dr.Delivery or the Customer do not comply with the shipping regulations.
