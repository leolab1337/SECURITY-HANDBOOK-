Rights of Customers and Dr.Delivery



  1. Rights of Customers
      Customers have the right to:

    1. Requiring Dr.Delivery to provide content-based services at the scope of service and other agreed service delivery needs.

    2. Be assured of private information confidentiality and safety for information in the form of documents, packages, packages of goods.

    3. Customers have the right to complain about damaged, torn, damaged, lost items. The statute of limitations for complaints is specified as follows:

      1. 06 months from the end of the postal processing time, for complaints about the loss of postal items or delayed delivery of the items compared to the announced transit time; where Dr.Delivery does not disclose the transit time, this period shall be counted from the day after the date of acceptance of the item;

      2. 01 month from the date the postal item is delivered to the recipient, for complaints about the item being lost or damaged, about the freight rate and other matters directly related to the item.

    4. To be compensated for damage as agreed upon and in accordance with current postal laws.

    5. Ask Dr.Delivery for monthly billing statements and bills.
