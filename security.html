Term of Security and Use Information



  1. Collection of personal information

    “Personal Information” means information about the Customer and is identifiable, including but not limited to name, nationality, address, phone number, fax number, banking information, credit card information, gender, residency status, financial status, personal preferences, email address of the Client, occupation, photo and any other information about the Client that the Client has provided to Dr.Delivery in registration forms, service registration forms.

    The provision of Customer's Personal Information is completely voluntary. However, if Customer does not provide Personal Information to Dr.Delivery, Dr.Delivery will not be able to process Customer's Personal Information for the Additional Purposes and Purposes as set forth below and may result in Dr.Delivery is unable to provide products or services to Customer or cannot accept payments made by Customer.

    For corporate customers, agents, and sellers, the provision of Customer's Personal Information is mandatory, Dr.Delivery may collect Customer's Personal Information from public information ources or from parties Tuesday.



  2. Purpose of using information

    Dr.Delivery may use and process Customer's Personal Information for the purposes of Dr.Delivery's business, transportation, delivery and freight forwarding, payment and other activities which may include, not limited for the following purposes:

    In case the Customer is a Customer using Dr.Delivery's services:

      To perform Dr.Delivery's obligations under any agreement entered into with the Customer;
      To provide any service requested by the Customer;
      To process Customer's installation and registration and provide services to Customer;
      When Customer requests to download and use the Dr.Delivery application or any other Dr.Delivery application, in order to process Customer's request, provide the Application to Customer, and enable Customer to use the Application.
      To process and manage Customer's accumulated points in the Loyalty program;
      To process Customer's participation in any event, activity, focus group, research, contest, promotion, poll, survey or product;
      To process, manage or verify Customer's subscription to Dr.Delivery products and services and to provide Customer with subscription benefits;
      To understand and analyze our sales and the needs and preferences of our Customers;
      To develop, improve and provide products and services that meet the needs of the Customer;
      To handle the exchange and return of goods.
