General Policy

The policy of our company is pretty simple: just follow the rules! We are convinced that focusing on quality, protecting the environment, striving for safety and awareness of the risks that arise for our customers, and at the same time for our company and employees, is an important factor for success in the global competition.

Therefore, the quality of our products and services, as well as attention to environmental and safety issues, are the highest priority for our company and an integral part of our corporate philosophy. At our core, we want to improve the services of our customers. To achieve this goal, which is part of our message and perspective, we have guided the system on 5 core principles:

  1. Customers

    The main principle of our company is a high level of satisfaction of our customers, which we achieve not only by high quality and provided services, but also by efficiently managed support processes. Timeliness, helpfulness and adaptability are integral parts of our entrepreneurial philosophy. The effectiveness of our own products is guided by cost control.

  2. Employee Development and Management

    Our employees are held accountable and motivated for the long term, with knowledge and experience that is systematically maintained and further improved through further education. Teamwork, effective communication, environmental awareness and safety awareness are our standards. The company's management expects employees to actively cooperate in ensuring environmental protection, strengthening information security and ensuring the safety of workplaces with regular checks.

  3. Technical and Technological solution

    Our products are based on modern knowledge and technology. We strive to provide quality services, constantly improving their level through trial and error. The high use value of products and services is also our key factor in a competitive environment.

  4. System and Process Approach

    We manage all processes in our company using a systematic method. Using project management and knowledge management, we not only optimize the flow of materials and information, but also activities in all other areas. Information security is based on the systematic approach of our company to any issue.

  5. Stability and Responsiblity

    Our company complies with legally binding regulations. We care about the maximum protection of information, both our own and those of customers, with an emphasis on ensuring the confidentiality of information, its integrity and availability in the main areas of the company's activities. To implement this policy, we regularly review the allocated funds and other resources. In this way, we prevent situations, the consequences of which could negatively affect the environment, the health of employees, property, information security, the viability of the company and its reputation.
